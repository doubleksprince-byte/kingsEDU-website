Lesson 2: What are the foundational steps to writing a summary?

The acronym MOREFEDS can help you write your summary. It stands for M-Minor details O-Opionions R-Repetition E-Examples F-Figurative Devices E-Expert Opionions D-Dialogue S-Statistics and tells you what to leave out of your summary.
